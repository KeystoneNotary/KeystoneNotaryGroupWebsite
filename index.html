We can notarize documents in any language, but all signers must be able to communicate directly with the notary in English to confirm their understanding and willingness to sign.

An acknowledgment verifies the signer's identity and that they signed willingly. A jurat requires the signer to swear or affirm the document's contents are true, and they must sign in the notary's presence.
